TNS combines classical triangulation with constructivist pedagogy. Unlike standard Q&A, models act as investigator proxies. Divergence is data. Core components: 3-Filter Model – Adoption (org), Qualification (human), Institutional (system); V3.3 Falsification Layer – every claim tested against failure conditions; Power Layer – explicit audit of control and profit. Repository structure: /protocol for P1-P8, /case_studies for applications. DNS implements this as executable protocol with six LLMs, Four Questions, and Δdiv metric.
